Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 July 2026  ·  Applies to: Mellon BuildKit

Overview

Mellon BuildKit is built by Sharp Mellon Construction Company as a free offline site companion — a material and cost calculator, a project phase guide, a budget tracker, and a notepad. This policy explains, in plain terms, what happens to your information when you use it.

The short version: nothing leaves your device, because nothing is sent anywhere. There is no server behind this app, and we have no way of seeing what you enter into it.

Information we collect

Mellon BuildKit does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, device identifiers, or analytics. We do not use cookies, trackers, advertising IDs, or third-party SDKs of any kind.

Anything you enter — project dimensions, budget entries, notes, or dates — is saved only in local storage on your own device. It is never transmitted to Sharp Mellon Construction Company or to any third party, and we have no access to it.

Permissions

Mellon BuildKit does not request any device permissions — no camera, location, contacts, storage, microphone, or notifications access beyond what your device's OS provides by default for local, on-device features.

Internet & offline behaviour

The app is designed to work fully offline. It does not require, and does not use, an internet connection to function. The cost calculator, phase guide, budget tracker and notepad all run entirely on your device using data bundled into or entered directly in the app.

Third parties

Because Mellon BuildKit sends no data anywhere, there is no data to share with advertisers, analytics providers, or any other third party. We do not sell, rent, or trade any information, for the simple reason that we never receive any.

Children's privacy

Mellon BuildKit does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all. The app is suitable for general audiences.

Data deletion

Since all data stays on your device, you can remove it at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the app — no request to us is needed.

Changes to this policy

If Mellon BuildKit's data practices ever change — for example, if a future version adds an optional online feature — we will update this policy and the in-app description before that version is released, and this page will reflect the change with a new effective date.
